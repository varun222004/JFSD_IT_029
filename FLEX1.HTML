NTA JEE Mains session 1 result announced at jeemain.nta.nic.in

Candidates who appeared for paper 1 can check the result notification and download their scorecards from the official website.

The National Testing Agency (NTA) on February 11, 2025, announced the result of the first session of the Joint Entrance Examination (JEE) Main 2025 at jeemain.nta.nic.in.

JEE Main is a national-level entrance examination held twice a year. Those who clear this test can apply for admission to undergraduate courses offered by the National Institutes of Technology (NITs), Indian Institutes of Information Technology (IIITs) and many other government and private technical education institutions. JEE Main also serves as the screening test for the IIT JEE Advanced. The top 2.5 lakh candidates from different categories to clear the JEE Main exam can appear for JEE Advanced.
